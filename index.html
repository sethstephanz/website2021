Seth Stephanz is a developer and writer. He is a recent graduate from the Iowa Writers’ Workshop and has years of experience in education; content creation and curation; and in development of accessible, mobile-responsive applications and sites.

Projects

YelpCamp

Built as the capstone of Colt Steele’s Web Developer Bootcamp, this project uses Node.js, Mongoose, and Express for routing and serving; BCrypt for authentication, authorization, and login; Bootstrap and EJS for front end and templating; Mapbox for data visualization and geolocation; and Mongo for CRUD operation. The result is a full-stack Yelp-esque project that allows log-in and log-out, logging and review of new entries, and editing and deletion capabilities.
